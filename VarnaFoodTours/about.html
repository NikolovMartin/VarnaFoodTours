LIVING LIKE A LOCAL

Varna Food Tours

It has always been during travelling that it fascinates me to explore the lives of the locals rather than being just “another tourist” on the main street. Wherever the wind has taken, there is always a set of questions whose answers need to be found in order to create a feeling and a long-lasting memory.

My name is Todor and I have had the chance to live in 4 different countries across 3 continents and explore a dozen cities in the past 9 years up to the point when I decided to go back and reconnect with my roots and share my passion for food and places with fellow explorers and food lovers.

Where do locals enjoy their food?
Where do they go to hang out?
Do these beautiful buildings have a story?
How was life in the past and how it has evolved?

Those are the real gems of a destination and I believe the ones who seek for such experience need to have the chance to live it. This is a quest is to share the culture behind with travelers, visitors, gastronomic enthusiasts and foodies.

The best way to learn about a new place is through putting your palate on a journey and let the flavors touch your soul. This tour experience has the sole purpose of introducing Varna and Bulgaria in a slightly different way, beyond of what is considered in the common status quo.
The places I have chosen are carefully hand -picked and my aim is to create an all-round gastronomic and urban introduction of Varna and inspire the continuous exploration of the gems the city has to offer to its guests.
