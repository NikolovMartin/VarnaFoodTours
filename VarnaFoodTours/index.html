Why Varna Food Tours

As locals, we are extremely passionate about our local food, our city and its history. As such, we love to share with you the best Bulgarian foods and its unique culture. By booking with us, you'll get to try delicious food and drink from across Bulgaria but more specifically from the Varna region, plus we will show you how to find the best shops and restaurants, and many other great local tips to make your experience truly memorable.

Your perfect 6 hours of walking, eating and wine tasting
in Varna

In this walking and tasting tour you will be able to get to know Varna as a culinary and historical destination. In my quest to represent the city and its culture in a slightly different way, I have chosen a considerable amount of food and drinks to be experienced, hence our tasting experience is a perfect full day activity. Believe or not, the 6 hours go very fast when you spend a whole day walking and trying different kind of food and drinks

What you have to know is that the places are carefully hand- selected and in each of them, I have sat down with the owners to devise the best possible menu to taste Bulgarian national cuisine. Because It`s all about culture...

Varna has an unique character that dates back to hundreds of thousands of years when the first signs of human life are found on the Balkan Peninsula. Since the city is already internationally recognized for its archaeological heritage, Varna Food Tours would like to portray the city through its emblematic buildings from the beginning of the 20th century. At this period of time the city went under a vast construction and planning. You will be able to taste a bunch of different traditional dishes made with local ingredients ranging from eating pastry on the street to having a private wine tasting experience.
